Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

Hi Fi PDF Tool Kit does not collect, store, or transmit your files. All PDF processing happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript.

2. File Security

Your files never leave your device. We do not upload files to any server. Files are processed locally and automatically removed from memory after processing.

3. Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your preferences (theme, recent tools, presets). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is collected.

5. Third-Party Services

We load Font Awesome, Google Fonts, and PDF processing libraries from CDNs. These services may log standard web access data.

6. Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking. Only essential localStorage is used for app functionality.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us via our Contact page.
